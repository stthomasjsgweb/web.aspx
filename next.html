About us


It has been glorious 25 years since we have started this journey of educating the society. This all started from a small but dear family of 25 students in a small building near Pahadi Mandir Jharsuguda. Our Principal madam Mr Sheeba Daniel was a first teacher there who laid the foundation of teaching in our school. Then because of the dedication and hard work of our Director Mr Shani Daniel and our Principal Mrs Sheeba Daniel , now our family has grown to be comprised of 1500+ students and is still growing. Here at ST. Thomas English School we make sure that our students are provided with best quality education and care.
